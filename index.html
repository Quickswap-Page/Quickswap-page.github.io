QuickSwap FAQ

What is quickswap?

QuickSwap is a decentralized exchange and DeFi app for token swaps, liquidity pools, farms, staking, and related trading tools across Polygon-centered EVM networks.

Is quickswap the official app?

This landing page is not the official app; it routes users to the official app at dapp.quickswap.exchange/swap.

Which networks does quickswap support?

QuickSwap documents support for Polygon PoS, Base, Polygon zkEVM, Ethereum, Immutable zkEVM, Manta Pacific, Soneium, X Layer, Dogechain, Somnia, MANTRA, and other app-listed markets.

Do I need a wallet for quickswap?

Yes. Swaps and liquidity actions require a compatible self-custody wallet, enough network gas, and your own review before signing transactions.

How do I avoid fake quickswap links?

Use the verified app domain, compare links with the official docs, and never approve wallet permissions from lookalike URLs.
